INTELLIPOINT

Designed IntelliPoint—the driver software that enables unique features of Microsoft mice. Users could customize settings, reassign buttons, and configure their mouse to fit their working style.

The challenge was making powerful customization accessible to both novice users who just want things to work and power users who want fine control.
