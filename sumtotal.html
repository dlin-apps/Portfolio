The Challenge

BUILDING UX FROM SCRATCH

SumTotal Systems provided e-learning and performance management web applications to enterprise customers. When I joined, there was no dedicated UX function. I started the first UX team at the company from scratch, building the team and discipline while changing corporate culture to be more user-centered.

I hired and managed a team across the US and India, establishing UX practices that would improve all SumTotal products.
