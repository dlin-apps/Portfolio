About

DESIGNING AT SCALE

I have been designing software and hardware user experiences for the past 25+ years. I am a multi-disciplinary global UX leader with a track record of building diverse and high-performing teams and delivering successful software and hardware products at scale.

I am a UX Designer and Researcher by heart with a passion for starting and building UX Design, Research, and User Assistance teams. I have 5 years experience serving on an executive team and 16+ years leading the UX discipline at companies including Microsoft, Qualtrics, Axon, and Workday. More recently, I've been focused on AI-enabled product design—leading AI/ML UX strategy at enterprise scale and exploring how AI can help people make better decisions.

I'm also an entrepreneur and builder. I co-founded Skill Cafe, an AI/ML-powered job matching platform, and I'm currently prototyping fintech tools exploring how AI can help people make better financial decisions. I teach UX to graduate students at Northeastern University to give back to the UX community and prepare the next generation of UX professionals.
